# Activities

Amateur radio offers a multitude of activities.

## Content
- **Contesting**: Competing for the highest number of contacts.
- **DXing**: Contacts with distant and rare countries.
- **Portable Operations**: SOTA (Summits), POTA (Parks), IOTA (Islands).
- **Satellites**: Communication via amateur satellites.
- **ARDF**: Amateur Radio Direction Finding ("Fox hunting").
