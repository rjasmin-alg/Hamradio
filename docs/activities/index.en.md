# Activities

Amateur radio offers a multitude of activities.

## Content
- [**Contesting**](contesting.en.md): Competing for the highest number of contacts.
- [**DXing**](dxing.en.md): Contacts with distant and rare countries.
- [**Portable Operations**](portable.en.md): SOTA (Summits), POTA (Parks), IOTA (Islands).
- [**Satellites**](satellites.en.md): Communication via amateur satellites.
- [**ARDF**](ardf.en.md): Amateur Radio Direction Finding ("Fox hunting").
