# Band Plans

A band plan is an agreement on the use of parts of amateur bands for different modes of operation. Respecting the band plan is a matter of amateur radio ethics and culture.

Here we present the plan for **IARU Region 1 (Europe)**, with notes for **Region 2 (North & South America)** where significant differences exist.

## HF (Shortwave)

"Top Band". Night band.

| 1838 - 1840 | Digital (FT8 @ 1840) | |
