# CB Radio (Citizens Band)

Citizens Band (CB) is a public radio service that does not require a license exam, but still has its own rules.

## Content

- [**Introduction to CB**](intro.en.md): History and purpose.
- [**Regulations and Channels**](channels.en.md): Allowed frequencies (27 MHz) and power limits.
- [**Equipment**](equipment.en.md): Radios, antennas, and accessories.

## Channel Table

CB uses 40 channels in the 27 MHz range. The most popular channels are **Channel 9** (emergency) and **Channel 19** (truckers/road).
