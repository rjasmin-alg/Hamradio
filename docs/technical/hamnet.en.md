# Hamnet (High Speed Multimedia)

Hamnet is a high-speed amateur radio network based on the IP protocol, using modified WiFi equipment on amateur radio frequencies.

## Content

### Intro & Infrastructure
What is Hamnet, how it works, and what equipment is used (Ubiquiti, Mikrotik).

### German Hamnet (DAeC)
Germany has one of the most developed Hamnet networks in the world. Here we will share their experiences and best practices.

### Applications
- Repeater linking
- Video surveillance
- VoIP telephony
- Access to servers and services within the network
