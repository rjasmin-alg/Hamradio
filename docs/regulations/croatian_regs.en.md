# Croatian Regulations

Amateur radio in Croatia is regulated by a series of laws and ordinances.

## Key Documents

### 1. Electronic Communications Act
The fundamental law governing the use of the radio frequency spectrum.

### 2. Ordinance on Amateur Radio Communications
This is the "bible" for hams in Croatia. It defines:
- License types and classes.
- Frequency bands and power limits.
- Technical conditions for stations.
- Call sign formation rules.

### 3. National Table of Frequency Allocations
The document that determines the purpose of each part of the spectrum in Croatia.

## Competent Authorities

- **HAKOM (Croatian Regulatory Authority for Network Industries)**: Issues licenses, allocates call signs, and monitors operation.
    - Web: [www.hakom.hr](https://www.hakom.hr/en)
- **HRS (Croatian Amateur Radio Association)**: The national association representing the interests of hams, organizing exams, and managing the QSL bureau.
    - Web: [www.hamradio.hr](https://www.hamradio.hr/)
