# Welcome to the Ham Radio Handbook

This is the central hub for all information regarding amateur radio. Whether you are a beginner looking to pass the exam or an experienced operator seeking specific technical details, you will find everything you need here.

## What is Amateur Radio?

Amateur radio (ham radio) is a popular hobby and service that brings people, electronics, and communication together. People use ham radio to talk across town, around the world, or even into space, all without the Internet or cell phones.
