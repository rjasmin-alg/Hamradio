# YOTA (Youngsters On The Air)

YOTA is an international initiative of IARU Region 1 aimed at attracting and retaining young people in amateur radio.

## Activities
- **YOTA Summer Camps**: Every year in a different country, gathering hundreds of young hams.
- **YOTA Month (December)**: Special call signs with the YOTA suffix are active all month. The goal is to make contact with them.
- **YOTA Contest**: A contest tailored for young people.

## YOTA Croatia
Croatia is very active in the YOTA program. Young Croatian hams regularly participate in camps and achieve top results in contests.

If you are under 26, this is the program for you!
