# Associations and Organizations

Radio amateurs are organized at national and international levels.

## HRS (Croatian Amateur Radio Association)
The umbrella organization of radio amateurs in the Republic of Croatia.
- **Membership**: You become a member through a radio club or directly.
- **QSL Bureau**: HRS runs the national QSL bureau for card exchange.
- **Exams**: Organizes license exams.
- **Contests**: Organizes 9A CW, 9A Activity, and other contests.
- [HRS Website](https://www.hamradio.hr/)

## IARU (International Amateur Radio Union)
The world organization representing radio amateurs before the ITU.
- Croatia is a member of IARU Region 1.
- [IARU Website](https://www.iaru.org/)
