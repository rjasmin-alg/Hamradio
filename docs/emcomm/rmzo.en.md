# RMZO (Emergency Radio Network)

RMZO is a system within the Croatian Amateur Radio Association that is activated in case of disasters.

## Organization
- **National Level**: Coordinated by HRS in cooperation with the Civil Protection Directorate.
- **Local Level**: Radio clubs and individuals who make themselves available to local headquarters.

## Activation
RMZO is activated at the call of competent authorities (112) or self-initiated in case of obvious telecommunication failure.

## Frequencies
- **3.675 MHz (80m)**: Main frequency for national coordination.
- **145.500 MHz (2m)**: Local simplex frequency.
- **Repeaters**: Local repeaters with independent power supply are used.
